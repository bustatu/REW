Enemies

The game contains 3 enemies

The turret

It shoots bulets in the direction it's facing. Each bullet deals 1 damage.

The strafer

It moves and bounces around. If he hits you, you die.

The zombie

He may look chubby, but when he runs, he's gonna catch you. And you die.
